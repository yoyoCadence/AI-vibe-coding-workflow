# Decision Log

Append-only. Newest entry on top. Record decisions that a future agent could
not re-derive from the code: architecture choices, rejected alternatives,
tradeoffs, "we tried X and it failed because Y".

Format:

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — T-001 — <decision title>
- context: why a decision was needed
- decision: what was chosen
- alternatives: what was rejected and why
- consequences: what this makes easier/harder
- author: <agent or human>
```
